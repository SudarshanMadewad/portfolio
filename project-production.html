PHOTO & FILM PRODUCTION HOUSE

Creative Agency Website

PROJECT

Photo & Film Production House

ROLE

Web Designer & Developer

TIMELINE

3 Weeks

TOOLS

Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

SKILLS
Web Design Visual Design Front-end Dev Motion Design
01 — PROBLEM

THE CHALLENGE

A growing photo and film production house needed a digital presence that matched the cinematic quality of their work. Their existing website was template-based, failed to showcase their portfolio effectively, and didn't convert visiting clients into inquiries.

02 — RESEARCH

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Analyzed leading production house websites and identified key patterns in how creative agencies present their film and photography work. Focused on immersive visual storytelling and minimal UI that lets the work speak for itself.

MOODBOARD & REFERENCES Visual Direction
03 — DESIGN

VISUAL EXECUTION

Created an editorial-style layout that treats each project as a cinematic story. Full-bleed imagery, dramatic typography, and smooth scroll transitions create an immersive browsing experience.
