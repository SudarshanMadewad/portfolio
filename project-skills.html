02 — PROJECT OVERVIEW

Problem Statement & Goal

The current application helps students track academic and 21st-century skills, but the experience feels outdated, information-heavy, and lacks motivation and engagement. Students struggle to quickly understand their progress, skill growth, rankings, and daily improvement opportunities.

Goal — Redesign the Skill Passport experience to:

  • improve usability
  • increase student engagement
  • create a modern and premium experience
  • make skill tracking easier to understand
  • encourage daily participation in LevelUp activities
  • improve visibility of skill growth and achievements
03 — PRODUCT UNDERSTANDING

What Is Skills App?

Skills App is a student skill development platform that helps students:

  • track 21st Century Skills
  • monitor semester progress
  • compare performance with peers
  • complete skill improvement tasks
  • earn badges and certificates
  • build a digital Skill Passport

05 — RESEARCH & AUDIT

Research Findings

01

82% of students feel more motivated when progress is visible.

Implemented: Progress Bars, XP System, Level System, Streak Tracker, Badges as rewards, Streak System.

02

Long reports are ignored. Students rarely read lengthy reports or observation text. Instead, they prefer visual summaries, charts, key insights, and actionable recommendations.

Design Opportunity: Transform reports into visual and digestible content.

03

Social recognition matters. Students enjoy sharing achievements on social media and with peers. Popular sharing content: Certificates, Rankings. They want a shareable passport with attractive badges and milestones.

Design Opportunity: Improve the shareable passport experience.

04

Gamification improves engagement and task completion.

Implemented: Levels, Badges, XP Rewards.

05

Visual dashboards improve information processing.

Implemented: Modern Dashboard, Skill Cards, Progress Analytics.

06

Motivation comes from rewards. Students are more likely to complete tasks when rewards are involved. Most desired rewards: Certificates, Badges, XP Points, Streaks, Ranking.

07

Students need actionable feedback.

Implemented: Skill Overview, Peer Comparison, Personalized Recommendations.

09 — DESIGN GOALS & PROCESS

Project Goal, Process, Tools

The goal of this redesign was to improve the overall user experience of the Skills App Skill Passport app by making it more modern, intuitive, engaging, and student-friendly.

My Process

  • studied the existing application screens and user manual
  • analyzed user flows and information architecture
  • restructured content hierarchy for better usability
  • redesigned screens with a focus on clarity, accessibility, and engagement
  • applied modern mobile UI patterns and UX principles

Tools Used

  • Figma — UI Design & Prototyping
  • ChatGPT — UX research, feedback, design review, and image generation
  • Stitch AI — layout exploration and design ideation
